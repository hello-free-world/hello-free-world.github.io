My Little Pony Season 5 Episode 1 and 2 is about fighting against collectivism. Every pony living in a village gave up their cutie marks and special talents, so they can achieve true friendship by being equal. But the equality is only a false appearance. In a secret talk, ponies said they desired their cutie marks back again but were scared by their leader for losing their power. In the end, ponies drove off their leader and returned to the village to celebrate getting their individuality back.

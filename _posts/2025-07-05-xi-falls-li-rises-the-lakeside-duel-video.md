Once every decade, the Central Assembly convenes to pass on leadership and restore balance to the martial world. But this time, Master Xi Jinlong of the Purification Sect breaks tradition—declaring before all sects that he will extend his rule indefinitely. The announcement sends shockwaves through the gathering.

Former Alliance Leader Hu Jingtang is still present, and a young rising swordsman, Li Keyun, watches from the hall.
A storm is brewing.

The martial world teeters on the edge of upheaval.

As the poison palm of tyranny spreads,
will the righteous ever unsheathe their swords again?

title: Dr. He's "Chinese Dream" (1 Video)
---

This animated short uses dark humor to portray the absurd story of Dr. He, a U.S.-educated scholar who returns to China full of patriotic passion, only to be crushed by the very system he devoted himself to. Through satire and irony, the film critiques institutional rigidity and invites reflection on loyalty, belief, and belonging.
